

The renal function panel is a test that is done to see how well your kidneys are working. The test measures the levels of different substances in your blood, including creatinine and urea. Creatinine is a waste product that is made by your muscles. Urea is a waste product that is made by your liver. The test also measures the level of potassium in your blood. Potassium is a mineral that is found in many foods.
